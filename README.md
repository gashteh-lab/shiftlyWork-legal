# shiftlywork-legal
Last updated: 3 May 2026
This Privacy Policy explains how Shiftly “the App" handles your information. Shiftly is developed and operated by Mohamad Gashteh, based in the United Arab Emirates.
By installing or using Shiftly, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

1. Our Approach in One Sentence
Shiftly is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not sell, rent, share, or monetise your personal information, and we do not run advertising or third-party analytics inside the app.

2. Information You Provide to the App
Shiftly stores the information you enter in order to function. This includes:
Your shift schedule, including dates, shift types, start and end times, and notes
Shift patterns, rotations, and saved templates
Annual leave allowance, leave entries, and balance configuration
Comp off, standby, and other custom leave types you create
Shift swaps and their status (pending, approved, completed, cancelled), including any colleague names you choose to enter
Payroll configuration (hourly rate, overtime threshold, night differential percentage, weekend premium, manual earnings entries)
App preferences such as theme, language, currency, weekend convention, and calendar start day
All of this information is stored locally on your device by default. It does not leave your phone unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync or use an export feature.

3. Health and Sleep Data
If you choose to enable Fatigue Guardian and grant permission, Shiftly reads sleep data from Apple Health (HealthKit) to calculate your fatigue score and sleep debt indicators.
In accordance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines:
Shiftly accesses HealthKit data only with your explicit permission, granted through the standard iOS permission prompt.
HealthKit data is used solely to calculate fatigue and sleep-related insights inside the app. It is not used for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to providing the feature you enabled.
HealthKit data is never transmitted to any server controlled by us or any third party. It is read on your device, used on your device, and not stored outside Apple's HealthKit framework.
You can revoke HealthKit access at any time from your iPhone's Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Shiftly.
If you do not enable Fatigue Guardian, no HealthKit data is read.

4. iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your Shiftly data is synchronised across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit framework.
Sync data is stored in your private iCloud container, accessible only to you through your Apple ID.
We do not operate any server that receives or stores your synced data. It travels through Apple's encrypted infrastructure between your devices.
You can disable iCloud sync at any time from inside Shiftly or from your iPhone's Settings →Mohamad Gashteh → iCloud.
If you do not enable iCloud sync, no data leaves your device.

5. Data We Do Not Collect
To be unambiguous, Shiftly does not collect any of the following:
Your name, email address, phone number, or government identifiers
Your location (the app does not request location permission)
Your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera (unless you explicitly attach an image to a note)
Browsing history or device identifiers for advertising
Crash logs or analytics through any third-party SDK
We do not embed Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Firebase Analytics, AppsFlyer, Mixpanel, or any similar tool.

6. In-App Purchases
If you purchase Shiftly Pro or any other paid feature, the transaction is processed by Apple via the App Store. We receive only the information Apple shares with developers (anonymous transaction confirmation and subscription status). We do not see your payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID.
Refer to Apple's privacy policy for how Apple handles payment information: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

7. Data Retention and Deletion
Because your data is stored on your device, it is retained for as long as Shiftly is installed.
You can:
Delete individual entries (shifts, leave, swaps) at any time from inside the app
Export your full data archive at any time from Settings → Data & Privacy → Export
Delete all app data at any time from Settings → Data & Privacy → Delete All Data
Uninstall the app, which removes all local data from your device
If iCloud sync is enabled, deleting data from one device removes it from your iCloud container and from your other synced devices.

8. Sharing and Disclosure
We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any third party. The only circumstances in which information may leave your device are:
When you choose to share an export (PDF, CSV, ICS) using the iOS share sheet
When you choose to enable iCloud sync (which uses your private Apple iCloud container, not our servers)
If required by a valid legal order, but since we do not have your data on our servers, we have no data to disclose

9. Children's Privacy
Shiftly is intended for adult shift workers. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in jurisdictions where that is the applicable threshold), and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used the app, please contact us so we can advise on data deletion.

10. Your Rights
Depending on where you live, you may have rights under data protection laws such as the EU GDPR, the UK GDPR, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).
Because Shiftly stores your data on your device under your control, you can exercise the most common rights — access, correction, deletion, and export — directly inside the app, without contacting us. For any other request related to your rights under applicable law, contact us using the details below.

11. Security
Local data on your device is protected by iOS encryption and your device's authentication (passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID). iCloud-synced data is protected by Apple's encryption in transit and at rest. We do not store passwords, authentication tokens, or payment information.
While no system is perfectly secure, we follow Apple's recommended practices for handling sensitive data on iOS and design the app to minimise the data that exists outside your device.

12. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice. Continued use of Shiftly after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
